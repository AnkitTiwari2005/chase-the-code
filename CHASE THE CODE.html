About CSI CUSB

The Computer Society of India Chandigarh University Student Branch (CSI CUSB) is dedicated to fostering technical skills, innovation, and collaboration among students. We organize hackathons, coding challenges, workshops, and industry interactions to empower future tech leaders.

Our mission is to create a thriving technical community where students can learn, grow, and innovate together. Through events like Chase the Code, we aim to develop problem-solving skills and foster teamwork in a competitive yet collaborative environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in Chase the Code?

Chase the Code has two separate events:

  • 1.0: Exclusively for 1st year students
  • 2.0: Designed for 2nd year students

This ensures a fair and exciting competition tailored to each year's experience level.

Do I need to form a team before registering?

Yes, forming a team is required for participation:

  1. Team size: Each team must consist of 3–5 members
  2. Register together: One member can initiate the registration and include all teammates

Since teamwork is the foundation of this event, collaboration is essential!

What should I bring to the event?

  • Student ID: For verification purposes
  • Water bottle: Stay hydrated during the competition
  • Notebook & pen: For brainstorming and taking notes
  • Enthusiasm! Come ready to solve problems and have fun

Will there be prizes?

Absolutely! We have exciting prizes for:

  • Top 3 teams in each event (1.0 and 2.0)
  • All participants will receive certificates of participation

Prize details will be announced closer to the event date.
